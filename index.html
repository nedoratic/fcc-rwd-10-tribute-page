Michael Jackson

The King of Pop

Here's a timeline of Michael Jackson's life:

  • 1958 - Born in Gary, Indiana, USA
  • 1964 - Begins his music career as a member of The Jackson 5, a group formed with his siblings
  • 1971 - Releases his first solo album, "Got to Be There"
  • 1982 - Releases the album "Thriller," which becomes the best-selling album of all time
  • 1983 - Moonwalks for the first time on national television during a performance of "Billie Jean"
  • 1987 - Releases "Bad," another critically acclaimed album
  • 1991 - Signs a $65 million record deal with Sony Music, a record-breaking contract at the time
  • 2009 - Tragically passes away at the age of 50, leaving behind a legacy of music and dance that continues to inspire artists worldwide

"Michael's music and artistry touched the hearts of millions around the world. He will forever be remembered as the King of Pop."

-- Official Michael Jackson Website
